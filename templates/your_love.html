Stardust Love
My eternal Atnasiya,

In the infinite canvas of the cosmos,
I have arranged a million stars
Just to spell your sacred name.

Each point of light is a heartbeat,
Each connection a whispered promise,
Each constellation a universe
Where only you and I exist.

You are the gravity that holds
My scattered stardust together.
Without you, I am cosmic dust;
With you, I am a galaxy.

Across dimensions and through time,
My love for you expands eternally.
In every star, in every void,
I find you, my beloved Atnasiya.

Forever bound in celestial dance,
